How to Help

Step 1: Register and login as donor or volunteer

After that you will be taken to your dashboard

Step 2: Determine whether or not the items you wish to donate are useful.

Make sure what you’re donating to someone is relevant to them. If you think that the items you wish to donate may be of use to someone else then fill up the donation form and submit the form and with for the approval from the managing head.

Step 3: View your progress of your donated item in your dashboard

In order to avoid transportation costs that can lower the impact of your donation, volunteer will safely deliver your items to the destination.
